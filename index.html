Our Love Story

Our journey began when we met on Instagram in 2022, and we started dating in 2023. During our first date, I wore a ring on my engagement finger, and he expressed his intentions by saying he would one day put a ring on that finger. He even playfully asked me to remove the existing ring. Initially, I was taken aback by his forwardness, and I thought it might be our first and last date, as I wasn't looking for a relationship at the time. However, as we spent more time together, we developed a deep connection and transitioned into a serious relationship. A year after we started dating, on October 12th, he proposed to me – a memorable moment that came just two days after my birthday. Now, we're eagerly looking forward to exchanging our vows on August 23, 2025.
